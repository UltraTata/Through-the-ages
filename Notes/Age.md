There are [[Timeline|6 ages]] in the game. Each age has...
- An [[Age objectives|age objective]] which [[Objective of the game|scores a goal]] when fulfilled
- Other secondary age [[Achievement|achievements]]
- A base [[Technology|technological tree]]
- One or more [[Leader|base decks]]
- A base [[Building|building pool]]
- A base [[Heritage|heritage pool]]
- A base set of [[Law|law questions]]

When passing to the next age, two things are conserved: [[Heritage]] and 3 [[Territory|territories]] at choice with no [[Building|buildings]] and 1 [[Population|population]] belonging to your [[Nation|nation]].
