Dawn of Civilization is the first [[Age|age]] of the game. It starts in 3150 BC with the unification of Egypt and ends in 2334 BC with the coronation of Sargon of Akkad.

There are two base [[Leader|decks]], a sedentary and a nomadic one. Dawn of Civilization [[Nation|nations]] must indicate which deck they use ("Sedentary nation" or "Nomadic nation").

==Sedentary base deck==
**Commander** x5: [[Profit]] iron
**Merchant** x5: [[Profit]] gold
**Queen** x5: [[Profit]] jade
**Priest** x5: [[Profit]] marble
**Raider** x1: [[Attack]] any nation. If successful, steal half its gold.
**Envoy** x3: *Reactive*. When attacked, Spend 1 jade per iron the enemy spent on the attack to cancel it (the enemy won't lose the iron but you will use the jade)
**Scribe** x5: [[Research]] 1 [[Technology|technology]] or adopt heritage spending marble
**Just king** x3: [[Pass law]] or adopt heritage spending jade.
**Builder** x5: [[Build]] a building or adopt heritage spending gold.
**Builder king** x1: Build as many buildings as you like or adopt heritage spending gold.
**Farmer** x5: Build as many farms as you like
**Peaceful king** x5: [[Grow population]]
**Expansionist king** x3: [[Migrate]] 1 population belonging to your nation per gold spent and [[Explore|explore]] 1 territory per iron spent. In that order.
**Vengeful King** x3: *Reactive*. Use after being attacked. Launch a [[Counterattack|counterattack]].
**Priest-king** x1: Assimilate 1 population under your control per marble spent.

==Nomadic base deck==
**Commander** x5: [[Profit]] iron
**Merchant** x5: [[Profit]] gold
**Queen** x5: [[Profit]] jade
**Priest** x5: [[Profit]] marble
**Raider** x5: [[Attack]] any nation. If successful, steal half its gold.
**Envoy** x3: *Reactive*. When attacked, Spend 1 jade per iron the enemy spent on the attack to cancel it (the enemy won't lose the iron but you will use the jade)

**Builder king** x1: Build as many buildings as you like or adopt heritage spending gold.
**Peaceful king** x5: [[Grow population]]
**Expansionist king** x3: [[Migrate]] 1 population belonging to your nation per gold spent and [[Explore|explore]] 1 territory per iron spent. In that order.
**Just king** x1: [[Pass law]] or adopt heritage spending jade.
**Explorer** x5: Explore 1 territory
**Migrant** x5: Migrate 1 population belonging to your nation per gold spent
**Migrant king** x1: Migrate all the populations belonging to your nations
**Conqueror** x5: Attack any nation. If successful conquer 1 territory
**Great conqueror** x1: Attack any nation. If successful conquer 1 territory. Repeat as many times as wanted. Only one time per nation.
**Hunter** x3: Add 1 population in any territory that you own
**Guerrilla commander** x1: *Reactive*. Use after being defeated. Nullifies the effect of the attack. If coins were spent, they are lost.
**Vengeful King** x3: *Reactive*. Use after being attacked. Launch a [[Counterattack|counterattack]].
**Priest-king** x1: Assimilate 1 population under your control per marble spent.

==Technological tree==
**Irrigation**: Costs 2 marble. Enables canal
**Writing**: Costs 2 marble. x2 marble profit
**Arithmetic**: Costs 2 marble. x2 gold profit
**Priesthood**: Costs 2 marble. x2 marble profit.
**Chronicles**: Requires Writing. Costs 2 marble. Add 5 Chroniclers to your reshuffling deck (Gain 1 jade every 2 marble spent)
**Masonry**: Costs 2 marble. Enables walls
**Long-distance trade**: Costs 3 marble. Enables caravans and add 3 explorer merchants (profit gold and explore 1 territory per gold spent)
**Bronze-working**: Costs 3 marble. x2 attack, x2 defense, enables blacksmith.
**Mining**: Costs 2 marble. Enables copper mine and tin mine.
**Crop rotation**: Costs 2 marble. +1 population growth per every pair of farms.

==General building pool==
**Farm**: Costs 4 gold. +1 [[Grow population|population growth]]
**Market**: Costs 8 gold. x2 gold profit
**Barracks**: Costs 8 gold. x2 iron profit
**Palace**: Costs 16 gold. x3 jade profit
**Temple**: Costs 8 gold. x2 marble profit.
**Palisade**: Costs 4 gold. +1 defense bonus
**Walls**: Requieres masonry. Costs 8 gold. +1 defense bonus per population.
**Caravans**: Requires long-distance trade. Costs 16 gold. x3 gold profit x3 marble profit
**Black-smith**: Requires Bronze-working. Costs 8 gold. For every pair of copper and tin, +1 attack and +1 defense

==Extractive building pool==
**Copper mine**: Requires mining. Costs 4 gold. Built on copper hills. Provides copper.
**Tin mine**: Requires mining. Costs 4 gold. Built on tin deposits. Provides tin.
**Canal**: Requires irrigation. Costs 8 gold. Built on rivers. +1 population growth to all territories with a farm

==Law==

**The question of authority**
Choice costs 2 jade
A) *Divine kingship*: x2 jade profit
B) *Kings as servants of God*: x2 marble profit

**The question of migration**
Choice costs 2 jade
A) *Welcome immigration*: +1 maximum population growth in all territories
B) *Forbid immigration*: x2 jade profits, Other nations can't migrate into your territories without your permission
C) *Forbid emigration*:
- /2 population growth
- Palisades and walls give +2 maximum population growth
- May not migrate into or add population in territories without population belonging to your nation

==Heritage==

**Cradle of Civilization**: Earned through achievements. Start every age with one technology of your choice.
**Ancient cities**: Earned through achievements. Start every age with one building of your choice.
**Raider bands**: Earned through achievements. x2 raiding earnings.

==Achievements==

**The Bronze Age**
Conditions: Have copper and tin while owning a black-smith.
Rewards: Adopt "Cradle of Civilization" heritage.

**Urbanization**
Conditions: Own a farm, walls, a market, a palace, a temple, and barracks.
Rewards: Adopt  "Ancient cities" heritage.

**Great Raiders**
Condition: Perform 6 successful raids.
Rewards: Adopt "Raider bands".
