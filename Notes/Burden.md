There are three types of burden, administrative, innovative and cultural. Administrative burden is a value that multiplies the price of [[Law|laws]]. Innovative burden multiplies the price of [[Technology|technology]], and cultural burden multiplies the price of [[Heritage|heritage]].

By default, the value of both burdens is equal to the population controlled by the player. However, because different effects may affect them, they are two distinct measures.

No burden may go below 1.
