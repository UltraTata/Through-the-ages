[[Civilization]]: Anatolia
[[Timeline|Age]]: [[Dawn of Civilization]]
Predecesor: None
Successor: [[Hitties]]

Base deck: Sedentary

==Heritage==

**Taru**: *Bull god of the weather.* Costs 3 marble. +3 marble profit per temple.
**Furushemu**: *Solar goddess.* Costs 3 marble. +3 marble profit per temple.
**Wurunshemu**: *Leopard guardian goddess.* Costs 3 marble. +3 marble profit per temple.
**Alaca Höyük bronze standards**: Costs 3 marble. By the time the age ends, take note of how many Alaca Höyük tombs there exist for the rest of the game. +1 marble per Alaca Höyük tomb.

==Technology==

**Trading colonies abroad**: Gained through achievements. x2 gold profit. Foreign nations may build Hattic Karums. Hattians may build Hattic Karums in foreign territory. These two require consent from both parts.
**Commercial diplomacy**: Gained through achievements. x2 jade. x3 jade in karums. Foreign nations may not conquer territories with one of their karum.
**Cultural dialogue**: Gained through achievements. x2 marble. May assimilate foreign population in territories with karums. May assimilate population in territories not under Hattic control if they posses a Hattic Karum.
**Coercive trade**: Gained through achievements. x2 iron. Does not require the consent of foreign nations for building Karums.

==Building==

**Karum**: *Foreign trading colonies.* Costs 8 gold. Must be built for another nation (For example "Akkadian Karum"). May be built by other nations within Hattian territory. Whether the Hattians or the foreigners build the Karum, it must be done with the consent of both nations. Foreign population belonging to the nation of the Karum doesn't count for the stability malus nor does it profit for the Hattians. Population belonging to the nation of the Karum does count for the stability bonus and for the profit for the nation they belong to. Population belonging to the nation of the karum may not be assimilated. x3 gold profit.
**Alaca Höyük tomb**: Requires Alaca Höyük bronze standards. Costs 4 gold. +1 marble profit.

==Leaders==

**Pamba**: *"Member of the alliance of 17 kings" Unique. Reactive.* Use upon being attacked. Force a neutral nation to side with you and send all of its iron to aid you.

==Achievements==

**Commercial Supremacy**
Conditions: Be the first nation to own a market.
Reward: Research "Trading colonies abroad".

**Political Supremacy**
Conditions: Be the first nation to own a palace.
Reward: Research "Commercial diplomacy".

**Cultural Supremacy**
Conditions: Be the first nation to own a temple.
Reward: Research "Cultural dialogue".

**Military Supremacy**
Conditions: Be the first nation to own barracks.
Reward: Research "Coercive trade".
