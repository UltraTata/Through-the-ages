==Heritage==

**Temple of Karnak**: Costs 3 gold. x2 marble.
**Temple of Luxor**: Costs 3 gold. x2 jade.
**Valley of the Kings**: Costs 3 gold. -1 gold cost for heritage (that costs gold).
**Temple of Ramses**: Costs 3 gold. Add 1 Ramesside King to your deck.
**Mortuary Temple of Hatshepsut**: Costs 3 gold. x2 gold.
**Obelisks**: Costs 2 gold. Envoys may use gold as well as jade.
**Tutankahamun's Sarcophagus**: Costs 2 gold. Start the [[Age of Empires]] with 8 gold.
**Prostration Formula**: *Open formula for letters written by vassals to Pharao.* Costs 2 jade. +1 jade profit per population controlled by a nation with both less population and less stability than oneself.
**Amarna Letters**: Costs 3 jade. Spend jade along side iron when defending.
**Atenism**: Requires either "Allow both cults" or "Forbid Amun-Ra" laws passed. Costs 2 marble. Add 5 Aten priests to your deck.

==Law==

**The question of Aten**
Choice costs 2 jade.
A) *Forbid Aten cult*: x2 jade. Enables "Atenism" heritage.
B) *Forbid Amun-Ra cult*: /2 cost for adopting heritage and passing laws. Enables "Atenism" heritage.
C) *Allow both cults*: x2 marble.

==Leaders==

**Habiru** x1: *Mandatory.* Start a Habiru crisis.
**Loyal vassal** x1: Pay as much jade or iron as the Habiru have strength to defeat them.
**Ahmose I**: *Unique.* Profit iron. Attack any nation, if victorious [[Seize|seize]] every territory with at least 1 Egyptian population. Repeat until defeated.
**Hatshepsut**: *Unique.* Profit gold. Explore a territory per gold spent. Build as many buildings as desired.
**Thuthmose III**: *Unique.* Profit iron. Attack a nation. If successful, seize 1 territory per iron spent.
**Amenhotep III**: *Unique.* Profit all coins. Adopt heritage.
**Nefertiti**: *Unique.* Profit jade twice.
**Seti I**: *Reactive. Unique.* Use after being defeated. Profit iron twice and attack the enemy. If successful, undo the effects of the war.
**Ramses I**: *Unique* Profit jade and iron.
**Ramses II**: *Unique.* Profit iron. Attack a nation. If successful, conquer 1 territory per iron spent.
**Ramses III**: *Reactive. Unique.* Used when under attack. Nullify the attack. Iron spent by the enemy is lost.
**Ramesside King** x0: Profit iron. Attack a nation. If successful, conquer 1 territory per iron spent.
**Aten Priest** x0: Profit marble. Pass law.

==Events==

**Habiru crisis**
The Habiru crisis starts at strength 1. Each turn it doubles. The crisis is ended by a loyal vassal.
-1 stability per point of Habiru strength.
