[[Civilization]]: Egypt
[[Timeline|Age]]: [[Dawn of Civilization]]
Predecesor: None
Successor: [[New Kingdom Egypt]]

Base deck: Sedentary

==Skills==

**Unification of Egypt**: Start the age with 9 [[Coins|jade]].

==Heritage==

**Pschent**: *"The Two Powerful Ones"* Costs 3 jade. x2 jade profit.
**Hieroglyphs**: Costs 3 marble. Requires Writing. x2 [[Coins|marble]] profit.
**Step Pyramid of Djoser**: Costs 3 gold. x2 gold profit.
**Red Pyramid**: Costs 4 gold. x3 marble profit.
**Pyramids of Giza**: Costs 4 gold. x3 jade profit.
**Great Sphinx**: *"Guardian of the spirits of the kings"* Costs 3 gold. x2 defense against nations with less jade than itself.
**Nome system**: Costs 3 jade. May bestow the title of "Nome" to nations which accept it. They will add 5 Haty-a to their deck. Nomes may chose to get rid of the title after defeating Egypt in battle. Egypt adds the jade profit of its nomes to its own.

==Technology==

**Early Espionage**: Costs 3 marble. Add 3 Early Spies.

==Leaders==

**Pyramid-building Pharao** x5: *"Got to prepare for the afterlife"*. Adopt heritage spending gold.
**Infrastructure-building Pharao** x5: Build as many buildings as you like paying jade instead of gold.
**Narmer**: *"Unifier of Lower and Upper Egypt" Unique.* Profit all 4 coins and adopt heritage.
**Djoser**: *Unique.* Research as many technologies as wished paying jade instead of marble.
**Pepi II Neferkare**: *"Longest reign in history, 90 years"*. *Unique*. Profit jade and adopt heritage. Chose weather to discard him or place him right back into the deck.
**Imhotep**: *Unique*. Watch next 3 card of the deck. Profit marble. Adopt heritage.
**Hemiunu**: *Unique*. Profit gold. Adopt heritage spending gold.
**Iry-pat** x5: Profit jade.
**Early Spy** x0: Learn the coins and reactions of a nation of your choice.
**Haty-a** x0: Profit jade. Egypt profits jade.
