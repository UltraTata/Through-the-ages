[[Civilization]]: Lebanon-Magreb
[[Timeline|Age]]: [[Dawn of Civilization]]
Predecesor: None
Successor: [[Sidon]]

Base deck: Sedentary

==Heritage==

**The Eternal City**: *Hosting our citizens since 5000 BC.* Costs 4 jade. May chose to not change nation upon advancing to the next age.
**Lebanese Cedar**: Costs 2 gold. x2 gold profit in forests.
**City of Books**: Costs 3 gold. May exchange gold for marble with other nations. Add 5 Book Sellers to your deck.
**Temple of Baalat Gebal**: *First monumental structure of the region.* Costs 3 gold. Each time you adopt a heritage while no other nation has adopted any heritage during the current age yet, adopt another heritage for free.
**Abishemu obelisk**: Costs 3 gold. Add to your profit an ammount of marble per player with more marble profit than you. The ammount is the difference between your marble profit and their own.

==Leaders==

**Rib-Hadda**: *Unique. Reactive.* Force a nation with both more population and stability to send all their attack points to your aid.
**Book Seller** x0: Profit 1 gold per marble spent or profit 1 marble per gold spent. No modifiers applied.
