[[Civilization]]: Lebanon-Magreb
[[Timeline|Age]]: [[Age of Kings]]
Predecesor: [[Byblos]]
Successor: [[Tyre]]

==Heritage==

**Eshmun**: Costs 3 marble. +3 marble profit per temple.
**Alexander Sarcophagus**: Costs 3 marble. Upon loosing defensively, earn 3 marble.
**Tabnit Sacophagus**: Costs 3 marble. Start the [[Age of Empires]] with 8 jade.
**Lycian Sarcophagus**: Costs 3 marble.

==Buildings==

**Key Port**: *Sidon was a port which connected the Aegean to Egypt and Mesopotamia.* Costs 8 gold. May perform jade payments replacing part of the cost with gold, up to 1 per population in this territory.
**Holy Field**: Costs 8 gold. +1 marble per temple. +1 maxpop growth in temples.

==Leaders==

**Zimredda**: *Unique. Reactive.* Use upon being helped by another nation in a war, x2 attack to the nations that intervene in Sidon's favor.
**Abdi-Milkuti**: *Unique.* Convert all marble to iron and attack a nation that controls population belonging to your nation. If succesful, sieze one territory with at least 1 population belonging to your nation.
**Eshmunazar I**: *Unique. Reactive.* Use when two nations other than Sidon fight. Intervene in favor of one of them. Earn 1 jade per point of attack contributed. If victorious, rob the defeated nation of half of its marble.
**Eshmunazar II**: *Unique.* Profit marble. Build as many temples as desired. Explore 3 territories.
**Bodashtart**: *Unique.* "The builder king" Convert all your jade into gold. Build as many buildings as wished.
**Abdashtart I**: *Unique.* 
**Tennes**: *Unique.*
**Tetramnestos**: *Unique. Reactive.* Intervene in a war and pay gold alongside iron to send attack points.
