==Heritage==

**Naturalistic Human Figures**: Costs 3 marble. +1 marble profit per heritage adopted.
**Victory Stele of Naram-Sim**: Costs 3 iron. Upon military victory (either offensive or defensive) profit iron and adopt heritage.
**Basetki Statue**: Costs 3 marble. x2 jade.
**Year Names**: Costs 3 marble. [[xA]] jade profit.
**Exaltation of Innana**: Costs 3 marble. x2 marble.

==Law==

**The question of Sumerian culture**
Choice costs 2 jade.
A) *Adopt Sumerian culture*: May adopt [[Sumer|Sumerian]] heritage.
B) *Synthesize Akkadian and Sumerian cultures*: x2 marble.
C) *Assimilate Sumerians*: +1 jade profit per population belonging to your nation. Add 1 Priest-king to your deck.

==Leaders==

**Sargon I**: *Unique.* Turn all jade into iron. Profit iron twice. Attack a nation, if successful, conquer 1 territory per iron spent. Attack other nations as many times as desired. May conquer up to 1 territory per nation.
**Enheduanna**: *"First named author of history" Unique.* Profit marble twice. Adopt heritage.
**Naram-Sin**: *Reactive. Unique.* Use when being attacked. Instantly defeat the attack. [[Seize]] 1 territory from the enemy.
