[[Civilization]]: Mesopotamia
[[Timeline|Age]]: [[Dawn of Civilization]]
Predecesor: None
Successor: [[Akkad]]

Base deck: Sedentary

==Heritage==

**Cuneiform Script**: Costs 3 marble. Requires Writing. x2 [[Coins|marble]] profit.
**Epic of Gilgamesh**: *"Seeking timelessness"* Costs 3 marble. +1 marble profit per heritage adopted.
**Great Ziggurat of Ur**: Costs 3 gold. x2 [[Coins|jade]] profit.
**Abramic priesthood**: Requires "Accept Abram's message". Costs 2 marble. Add 5 Abramic priests to the deck.
**Sumerian King List**: Costs 3 marble. [[xA]] jade profit.
**Ekur**: Costs 3 gold. Add 5 Nippur priests to the deck.
**E-Shumesha**: Costs 3 gold. x2 marble profit.
**Stele of the Vultures**: Costs 3 iron. x2 iron.
**White Temple**: Costs 3 gold. x2 marble profit.
**Lament about the fall of Lagash**: Costs 3 marble. Upon losing a defensive war, profit marble.

==Technology==

**Low-quality pottery**: Costs 2 marble. -1 building cost per population within a territory.

==Law==

**The question of Abram's message**
Choice costs 2 jade.
A) *Accept Abram's message*: Enables "Abramic priesthood" heritage.
B) *Guarantee Abram's followers freedom of religion*: x2 marble profit.
C) *Banish Abram!*: x2 jade profit.

==Leaders==

**Ea-Nasir**: *"Bad copper merchant" Unique.* Rob a nation of half their [[Coins|gold]] (rounded up). For every copper available, do it again.
**Urukagina of Lagash**: *"Offence there was none in Urukagina" Unique.* Profit jade and pass as many laws as wished.
**Messanepada**: *"King of Ur and Kish" Unique.* Attack a nation of your choice. If successful, [[Seize|seize]] one of their territories and rob it of all its jade.
**Lugalzagesi**: *Unique.* Attack as many nations as you wish. Afterwards, spends all the iron remaining conquering one territory per iron spent.
**Nippur priest** x0: Earn 3 jade per building owned in territories where at least 1 population belongs to your nation.
**Abramic priest** x0: Profit marble twice.
