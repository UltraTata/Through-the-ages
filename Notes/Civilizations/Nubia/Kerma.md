[[Civilization]]: Nubia
[[Timeline|Age]]: [[Age of Kings]]
Predecesor: [[Ta-Seti]]
Successor: [[Kush]]

==Skills==

**Kerma City**: If there is a single most populated territory. x3 jade profit on it and +1 jade profit per claimed or owned territory.

==Heritage==

**Control over the 4 cataracts**: Gained through achievements. /2 cost of building in rivers.
**Jebel Barkal**: Gained through achievements. When exploring, earn 1 iron per territory explored. Apply iron profit modifiers. Can't spend the iron on the same turn as it is earned.
**Kerma Tumuli**: Costs 3 marble. By the time the age ends, take note of how many Kerma Tumuli there exist for the rest of the game. +1 marble per Kerma Tumulus.
**Kerma Mercenaries**: Costs 3 iron. May sell or gift Nubian Mercernaries on hand at any moment.
**Royal Cattle**: Costs 3 jade. +3 jade profit per farm.
**Animalistic Religion**: Costs 3 jade. +1 marble profit per claimed or owned territory.

==Buildings==
**Kerma Tumulus**: *Kerma culture graves.* Costs 4 gold. +1 marble profit.

==Achievements==

**Control over the 4 cataracts**
Conditions: Own 4 river territories.
Reward: Adopt "Control over the 4 cataracts" heritage.

**Discovery of Jebel Barkal**
Conditions: Own 6 territories including 1 mesa.
Reward: Adopt "Jebel Barkal" heritage.
