Refers to taking a territory from another player and destroying all its buildings. 

By default, one may not...
1. Any territory with at least 1 population belonging to your nation may be conquered
2. Conquer a territory with two buildings if there are territories with one building available.
3. Conquer a territory with one building if there are territories with no buildings available.

The priorities follow the order of the list. The rules may be broken if both parties agree.

==Case 1==
New Kingdom Egypt has these territories:

1. 1 Egyptian
2. 1 Egyptian
3. 1 Egyptian
4. 2 Kermans

If Akkad won an attack with a conqueror, they can conquer any territory.

==Case 2==
New Kingdom Egypt has these territories:

1. 2 Egyptian, 1 Akkadian, a farm
2. 1 Egyptian
3. 1 Egyptian

If Akkad won an attack with a conqueror, they can conquer any territory.

==Case 3==
New Kingdom Egypt has these territories:

1. 2 Egyptian, 1 Akkadian, a farm
2. 1 Egyptian, 1 Akkadian
3. 1 Egyptian

If Akkad won an attack with a conqueror, they can only conquer territories  1 or 2.

==Case 4==
New Kingdom Egypt has these territories:

1. 1 Egyptian, a farm
2. 1 Egyptian
3. 1 Egyptian
4. 1 Egyptian, 2 Kermans

If Akkad won an attack with a conqueror, they can conquer any territory except 1.

==Case 5==
New Kingdom Egypt has these territories:

1. 1 Egyptian, a farm
2. 1 Egyptian, a market and a copper mine

If Akkad won an attack with a conqueror, they can conquer only conquer territory 1.
